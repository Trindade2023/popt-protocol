### Nomenclature Refinement

This addendum refines the physical terminology used in the foundation document:

1.  **Original Term (v8.1.2):** "Atomic Synchronization" (referring to local node hardware).
2.  **Corrected Term (v8.1.3):** "Piezoelectric Drift Analysis".

**Justification:**
Local hardware oscillators (Quartz XTAL) operate via the **Piezoelectric effect**. The term **Atomic** is strictly reserved for the External Time Reference (GPS/UTC) used as the benchmark.

The core logic of Proof of Drift and the Reputation Formula ($R$) remain unchanged.
